kazi nazrul islam
The National Poet of Bangaladesh


Rebel Poet


Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangaladesh who is especially reconized as a Bidrohi Kobi arround the country. Nazrul is famous poet , musician and philosopher of Bangaladesh. The famous poet Nazrul writing deals with the revolution of the British government



Awesome Writer


Nazrul writings explored about themes such as love, freedom, and revolution. He was against all bigotry, including religious and gender. Throughout his career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but is best known for his poems.
